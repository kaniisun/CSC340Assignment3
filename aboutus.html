About Us!

Smiski Lovers is a small business owned solely by me, Kanika Sun. I am a university student located in Greensboro, North Carolina. I became a Smiski collector last year and have loved everything about them! Though, I have struggled a lot trying to collect the Smiskis that I want and aim to collect a lot of the series and complete them. In order to combat that, I made this business in order to not only help myself but to also help others who are struggling as well!
If you're looking to buy, sell or trade Smiskis, please visit our Contact page and contact me about your inquires.
